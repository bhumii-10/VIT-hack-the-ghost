# 🗣️ SankatSaathi Multi-Lingual Voice Commands

SankatSaathi supports voice control in **English**, **Hindi (हिन्दी)**, and **Marathi (मराठी)**.

## 👥 Essential Commands (User)

| Goal | English Command | Hindi Trigger | Marathi Trigger |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **SOS Page** | `Emergency Help` | `आपातकालीन सहायता` | `तात्काळ मदत` |
| **Ambulance**| `Call Ambulance` | `एम्बुलेंस बुलाओ` | `रुग्णवाहिका बोलवा` |
| **Fire** | `Call Fire Brigade` | `दमकल केंद्र` | `अग्निशमन दलाला बोलवा` |
| **Dashboard**| `Go to Dashboard` | `डैशबोर्ड पर जाएं` | `डॅशबोर्डवर जा` |
| **News** | `Show news` | `समाचार दिखाओ` | `बातम्या दाखवा` |

## 🤖 NEW: Autonomous Hands-Free Mode
You can now operate the entire system without touching your mouse or keyboard.

1.  **Activate:** Click the **Brain Icon** 🧠 in the Navbar.
2.  **Wake-Word:** Say *"Hey Sankat Saathi"* followed by your command.
3.  **Language Detection:** The system automatically understands your choice from the dropdown. 
4.  **Example (Hindi):** *"Hey Sankat Saathi, दमकल केंद्र"*
5.  **Example (Marathi):** *"Hey Sankat Saathi, डॅशबोर्डवर जा"*

---

### 💡 Pro Tips for Best Performance
*   **Wait for the Sound:** Wait for the low "listening" chirp before speaking.
*   **Wake-Word First:** Always start with *"Hey Sankat Saathi"* for autonomous mode.
*   **Clear Speech:** Results are best in a quiet environment.
